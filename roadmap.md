# Easy Invoice Builder — Roadmap

We're constantly working to make Easy Invoice Builder better for small businesses. 
Here's what we're currently planning for upcoming releases.

---

## Coming Soon

**Full Accessibility**  
Comprehensive VoiceOver support and accessibility improvements to ensure Easy Invoice 
Builder works great for everyone.

**Spanish Language Support**  
A fully localized Spanish version of the app for Spanish-speaking users and businesses.

**Invoice Numbering by Customer**  
Assign and track invoice numbers on a per-customer basis for better organization 
and record keeping.

**Additional Sorting Options for Completed Invoices**  
More ways to sort and filter your completed invoices so you can find what you need faster.
